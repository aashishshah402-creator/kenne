RECLAIMING ANCIENT BEAUTY WISDOM

Himalayan Herbal Skincare Secrets: Reclaiming Natural Skincare in Nepal

Why Kennebis Started: Rescuing Himalayan Beauty Secrets

For centuries, high-altitude mountain communities possessed deep wisdom about the Himalayan beauty secrets of dermal health. They cold-pressed wild-growing hemp in the valleys of Karnali, creating pure hemp seed oil skincare remedies to protect wind-chafed skin. In Palpa, they hand-harvested native Chuiri seeds for rich, protective butter. In Dolpa, pure yak ghee and wild herbs healed damaged skin barriers. These local ingredients were not mere cosmetic choices—they were the foundation of natural skincare in Nepal, time-tested and highly effective.

However, the rise of commercial industrialization flooded store shelves in Kathmandu with mass-produced, chemically induced skincare. Aggressive chemical agents, petroleum byproducts, cheap mineral oils, parabens, and toxic synthetic fragrances dominated the market, imported by foreign conglomerates. Through intensive pseudo-scientific marketing campaigns, traditional knowledge was dismissed as "primitive" or "outdated" to convince people that synthetic laboratory-created products were superior. This pseudo-scientific care led to chronic skin barrier disruption and toxic chemical buildup, leaving Nepali skin vulnerable.

We saw our community sacrificing skin health to harsh synthetic formulas that were never designed for our climate. This is why Kennebis.hemp was founded: to rescue long-lost high-altitude botanical secrets from chemical extinction. Today, we stand among the leading Nepali skincare brands, backing ancient wisdom with modern dermatological science to formulate the best organic skincare in Nepal.

THE CHRONICLES OF KENNEBIS

Our Journey: 2022 to 2026

2022

The Seed of Rebellion

Disillusioned by the influx of toxic chemical cosmetics, our founding members met in Kathmandu. We recognized that while Nepal exported high-quality botanical raw materials, our own store shelves were filled with imported chemical products. We set out to change the narrative, starting formulation experiments in a tiny kitchen, focusing on hemp seed oil skincare and raw plant emollients.

2023

Sourcing the High-Altitude Treasures

We traveled to the remote highlands of Karnali and Palpa. We established direct relationships with local farmers and women-led agricultural cooperatives. By paying fair-trade premium prices, we secured the supply of wild-harvested hemp seeds and Chuiri fruit, supporting rural families while ensuring ingredient authenticity.

2024

Laboratory Validation & Launch

We opened our dedicated lab in Kathmandu. By combining ancient Ayurvedic practices with modern cosmetic science, we validated our cold-press extraction techniques to preserve the bioactives in our ingredients. We officially launched Kennebis, introducing Nepal's first science-backed, zero-chemical skincare line, establishing ourselves as pioneers among Nepali skincare brands.

2025

Pioneering the "Skincare You Can Eat" Standard

We took our zero-chemical philosophy to the extreme. We formulated a standard where our core formulas are composed entirely of edible-grade, nourishing lipids and botanicals, completely free of industrial synthetic preservatives. Our products gained nationwide acclaim as the premier skin barrier repair cream in Nepal.

2026

Leading Nepal's Ethical Beauty Movement

Today, Kennebis.hemp stands as Nepal's premier ethical beauty manufacturer. Supporting over 100 rural farming households across the country, we are expanding our zero-chemical skincare ecosystem, demonstrating that genuine Himalayan beauty secrets can outperform any synthetic chemical import.

About Kennebis Hemp

What is Kennebis Hemp?

Kennebis Hemp is a Nepal-based herbal skincare brand dedicated to creating handcrafted skincare products using carefully selected natural-derived ingredients, including plant oils, botanical extracts, herbal infusions, butters, and waxes. Our goal is to provide effective, nature-inspired skincare while maintaining high standards of quality and transparency.

Are your products natural?

Yes. Our products are formulated using natural-derived ingredients sourced from plants and botanicals. Each product page includes a complete ingredient list for full transparency.

Are your products handmade?

Yes. Our products are handcrafted in small batches to help maintain quality, freshness, and consistency.

Are your products lab tested?

Yes. We conduct laboratory testing on our products to ensure quality and safety standards. Our products are tested through recognized laboratories in Nepal and India, including Nepal Academy of Science and Technology (NAST) and Fare Labs Private Limited, India.

Do your products contain artificial fragrances?

No. Kennebis Hemp products do not contain artificial fragrances. Any scent present in our products comes naturally from botanical ingredients, plant oils, herbal infusions, extracts, or essential oils used within the formulation.

Do you test on animals?

No. Kennebis Hemp does not test finished products on animals.

Product Information

Are your products suitable for sensitive skin?

Many of our products are formulated with gentle ingredients suitable for sensitive skin. However, every individual's skin is unique. We recommend reviewing the ingredient list and performing a patch test before full use.

Can I use your products during pregnancy or breastfeeding?

If you are pregnant, breastfeeding, or under medical supervision, we recommend consulting your healthcare professional before introducing any new skincare products into your routine.

How long does it take to see results?

Results vary depending on the individual, skin type, skin condition, and consistency of use. Skincare improvements generally require regular and consistent use over time.

Why does my product look slightly different from a previous purchase?

Because we use natural-derived ingredients, slight variations in color, aroma, and texture may occur between batches. These natural variations are normal and do not affect product quality or performance.

What is the shelf life of your products?

Most products perform best when used within 6–12 months after opening. Please refer to the product packaging for specific guidance.

How should I store my products?

Store products in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight, excessive heat, and moisture. Always keep containers tightly closed after use.
