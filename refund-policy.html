Refund, Return & Cancellation

At Kennebis Hemp, we are committed to providing high-quality handcrafted herbal skincare products made with carefully selected natural-derived ingredients. Due to the personal-use nature of skincare products, certain restrictions apply to returns, exchanges, and cancellations to ensure product safety, hygiene, and quality.

By placing an order with Kennebis Hemp, you agree to the terms outlined below.

Returns

For hygiene and safety reasons, we do not accept returns of products that have been opened, used, tampered with, or altered after delivery.

A return request may be considered only under the following circumstances:

  • You received an incorrect product.
  • The product arrived damaged during transit.
  • The product was defective upon arrival.
  • The product received differs significantly from the item ordered.

To request a return, customers must contact Kennebis Hemp within 48 hours of receiving the order and provide: name, order number, contact phone, clear photographs of product and packaging, and description of the issue. Failure to report the issue within 48 hours of delivery may result in the return request being declined.

Approved returns must be sent back in their original, unused, unopened condition, including all original packaging where applicable.

Exchanges

We are happy to replace products when an issue is verified by our team. Exchanges are available only in the following cases:

  • Incorrect product delivered
  • Product damaged during shipping
  • Confirmed manufacturing or packaging defect

Once the issue has been reviewed and approved, Kennebis Hemp will arrange a replacement product or an appropriate alternative, subject to product availability.

Non-Returnable and Non-Exchangeable Items

The following products are not eligible for return or exchange:

  • Opened or used products
  • Products with broken safety seals
  • Products damaged due to improper storage or customer handling
  • Products purchased during clearance, promotional, or special sale events unless defective
  • Products returned without prior approval from Kennebis Hemp or outside the reporting period
  • Gift items and complimentary products

Natural Product Disclaimer

Kennebis Hemp products are handcrafted using natural-derived ingredients. Variations in color, aroma, texture, botanical appearance, or consistency may occur between batches. Such natural variations do not constitute defects and are not valid grounds for return, exchange, or refund.

Allergies and Individual Skin Reactions

While we formulate our products with skin compatibility in mind, individual skin types and sensitivities vary. Customers are responsible for reviewing ingredient information and performing a patch test before full use.

Kennebis Hemp cannot accept returns, exchanges, or refunds based on: personal/fragrance/texture preference, expected results, or individual skin sensitivities/allergic reactions. Unless a manufacturing defect is confirmed, these circumstances are not considered product faults.

Refunds

Refunds may be issued only when a return request has been approved or a replacement product is unavailable.

Approved refunds will be processed through the original payment method whenever possible. Refund processing times may vary depending on the payment provider. Shipping fees are generally non-refundable unless the issue resulted directly from an error made by Kennebis Hemp.

Cancellation Policy

Orders may be cancelled before they have been dispatched for delivery. To request an order cancellation, contact Kennebis Hemp through any of our official communication channels (Email, WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram) and provide your name, order number, and contact phone.

Once an order has been dispatched, shipped, or handed over to a courier partner, cancellation requests can no longer be accepted. Kennebis Hemp reserves the right to refuse cancellation requests for orders that have already entered fulfillment, packaging, or shipping stages.

Limitation of Liability

Kennebis Hemp's liability for any approved claim shall be limited solely to the replacement, exchange, or refund value of the purchased product. We shall not be liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, or special damages arising from the use or misuse of our products.
