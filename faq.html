Frequently Asked Questions

About Kennebis Hemp

What is Kennebis Hemp?

Kennebis Hemp is a Nepal-based herbal skincare brand dedicated to creating handcrafted skincare products using carefully selected natural-derived ingredients, including plant oils, botanical extracts, herbal infusions, butters, and waxes. Our goal is to provide effective, nature-inspired skincare while maintaining high standards of quality and transparency.

Are your products natural?

Yes. Our products are formulated using natural-derived ingredients sourced from plants and botanicals. Each product page includes a complete ingredient list for full transparency.

Are your products handmade?

Yes. Our products are handcrafted in small batches to help maintain quality, freshness, and consistency.

Are your products lab tested?

Yes. We conduct laboratory testing on our products to ensure quality and safety standards. Our products are tested through recognized laboratories in Nepal and India, including Nepal Academy of Science and Technology (NAST) and Fare Labs Private Limited, India.

Do your products contain artificial fragrances?

No. Kennebis Hemp products do not contain artificial fragrances. Any scent present in our products comes naturally from botanical ingredients, plant oils, herbal infusions, extracts, or essential oils used within the formulation.

Do you test on animals?

No. Kennebis Hemp does not test finished products on animals.

Product Information

Are your products suitable for sensitive skin?

Many of our products are formulated with gentle ingredients suitable for sensitive skin. However, every individual's skin is unique. We recommend reviewing the ingredient list and performing a patch test before full use.

Can I use your products during pregnancy or breastfeeding?

If you are pregnant, breastfeeding, or under medical supervision, we recommend consulting your healthcare professional before introducing any new skincare products into your routine.

How long does it take to see results?

Results vary depending on the individual, skin type, skin condition, and consistency of use. Skincare improvements generally require regular and consistent use over time.

Why does my product look slightly different from a previous purchase?

Because we use natural-derived ingredients, slight variations in color, aroma, and texture may occur between batches. These natural variations are normal and do not affect product quality or performance.

What is the shelf life of your products?

Most products perform best when used within 6–12 months after opening. Please refer to the product packaging for specific guidance.

How should I store my products?

Store products in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight, excessive heat, and moisture. Always keep containers tightly closed after use.

Orders and Payments

How do I place an order?

You can place an order directly through our website by selecting your desired products and completing the checkout process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently offer Cash on Delivery (COD) as our payment method. Customers can pay for their orders upon delivery.

Can I modify my order after placing it?

If your order has not yet entered processing or shipping, we may be able to assist. Please contact us as soon as possible through our official communication channels.

Can I cancel my order?

Yes. Orders may be cancelled before they are dispatched. Once an order has been shipped or handed over to the courier, cancellation is no longer possible.

Shipping and Delivery

Where do you ship?

We currently ship throughout Nepal.

How long does delivery take?

Estimated delivery times are:
• Kathmandu Valley: 1–3 business days
• Major Cities: 2–5 business days
• Remote Areas: 4–10 business days

Delivery times may vary depending on location, weather conditions, public holidays, and courier operations.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has been dispatched, delivery updates or tracking information will be shared through the contact details associated with your order.
